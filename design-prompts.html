Welcome to the Ideation Generator!

This tool helps you create random combinations of words to spark new design ideas.

  • Enter a number to say how many prompt cards to create, then click Generate.
  • Use the Admin button (top right, password hint: New Cross PGCE University) to customise the word lists for your own projects.
  • To revert all lists to their original defaults, open the Admin panel and use the 'Reset ALL to Default' button.
  • Your custom word lists are saved automatically in your browser. Use the Save/Load Lists buttons to keep different versions for classes or projects.
  • Click Print to PDF to create a shareable copy of your generated prompts.
